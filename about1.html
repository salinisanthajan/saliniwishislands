Let us create...

Wish Island leads in luxury resort and island development, crafting unique experiences that merge natural beauty with sustainability. Expanding globally, Wish Group is developing exclusive Wish Islands in ten locations: Maldives, Cyprus, The Bahamas, Sri Lanka, Fiji, Mauritius, Indonesia, Australia, Ecuador, and Eritrea.

Our Vision

To be the world’s leading network of luxurious Eco-resorts, where guests experience unparalleled adventure, tranquility, and cultural richness, all while contributing to the preservation and enhancement of the planet’s most pristine environments.

Our Mission

Crafting Exclusive Sanctuaries:

To design and operate luxurious island retreats that seamlessly blend with nature, offering unparalleled experiences that captivate the senses and nourish the soul.

Connecting People and Place:

To inspire profound connections between our guests and the vibrant ecosystems and rich cultural heritage of each location, creating meaningful and transformative journeys.

Championing Sustainability:

To leave a lasting, positive legacy through sustainable practices that enrich local communities, preserve natural beauty, and foster harmony between luxury and environmental stewardship.

Commitments

Integrity is the bedrock of Wish Group’s philosophy.


Core Values

Ethical Operations: Conducting all business activities with honesty, integrity, and fairness.


Accountability: Taking responsibility for our actions and their impact on the environment and communities


Transparency: Maintaining open, honest, and transparent communication with all stakeholders.

Commitment to Sustainability

Respect: Treating all individuals with respect and dignity, fostering a culture of inclusivity and equality.


Environmental Stewardship: Following best practices in social governance

Positive Impact: Ensuring resorts benefit both society and the environment.
